Now you can access Jetsom’s Top Rated eBay Consignment services and Stellar Customer Service from ANYWHERE!

​With our new Virtual Seller™ program, we are opening up our end-to-end eCommerce services to everyone in the continental United States as well as select international locations around the world*! The basics are the same, but with Virtual Seller™, we arrange for the shipment of your items right to our warehouse facility in Northern Virginia. We even pay the shipping costs**!  Once we receive your items, we proceed as usual: researching, photographing, pricing, and listing your items for sale. Once the sold item ships, we send you payment! It’s that simple!
